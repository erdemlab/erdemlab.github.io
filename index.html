Home

Our newly established lab is located at Umeå University and is affiliated with SciLifeLab. We are part of the national program for data-driven life science (DDLS), funded generously by the Knut and Alice Wallenberg Foundation. Our lab aims to merge machine learning and mechanistic computational models with patient data to create clinically predictive computational models for cancer.
